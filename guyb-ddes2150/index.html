Library Simulation


Many people who use libraries are unaware of the facilities available to them. Due to this, when going to libraries they often stick to what they know: only using the computers, or only borrowing books.

This simulation generates a person who knows a set amount about the facilities of this library, and then has them complete tasks according to what library facilities they know about.

Visitors with a higher knowledge score are more likely to know to scan the QR code at the door, as well as use the lesser-known library services.
