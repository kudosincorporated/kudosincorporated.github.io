    Forager, a game by KudosInc (Guy)

    Forager is the third "completed" game I've made using Javascript. It also uses the popular JS library jQuery.
    I made the game to expand my Javascript skills after becoming adept at CSS and HTML.
    The story so far:
    You have crash landed your tiny plane at the edge of a cliff, next to an dense forest. Frantically, you flee your craft in case it topples over the edge. Now, you must forage to survive while you walk deeper into the forest to finally find safety.
    Some simple tips/a guide:
    1. Use the 'look around' button to forage for some supplies.
    2. Click these supplies to add them to your inventory. Once an item is in your inventory, different actions are unlocked as you can combine items, use them, or leave them be until you find another combination.
    3. As you complete tasks, your skill in that respective task increases.
    4. Keep foraging and you gain the ability to find different types of items. There are currently 21 obtainable items.
    Important thing to note:
    This is an unfinished game. Currently there is no prestige mechanic, althought it possible to "beat" the game. I've tried to engineer the code in such a way that it would be faily easy to add new content -- but the game as it stands is just a slightly elevated proof of concept of the mechanics involved.
