10yearbasement

You are forced to stay in a basement for 10 years, after which you will be paid $10 million. You have 30 points to spend on items/amenities. There is no escape. The only supplies provided are a mattress, sink, toilet and unlimited toilet paper. These will not break down or require repair.

Putting at least one point into a column unlocks another room for the house, for a total of 8 rooms.
