This is an incremental game in three parts.

In it, you play as an intrepid new social media platform.

Part 1
"Humans are a resource."

You are an intrepid social media startup. As a fledgling organisation, the first order of business is to manually recruit users.

The platform can grow through word of mouth. The more users on the site, the faster additional users generate. Also increasing slowly is the platform's notoriety.
