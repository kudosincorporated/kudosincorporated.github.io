FAQ

Questers is a barebones incremental game I made to learn javascript. It uses jQuery, Bootstrap and FontAwesome as additional libraries.

A quick overview:
  • Clicking gives acclaim.
  • Clicking gives gems.
  • Clicking gives items.
  • Clicking hurts you.
  • Clicking hurts enemies.
  • Hurting enemies gives coins.
  • Coins buy potions.
  • Coins buy abilities.
  • Items give gems.
  • Gems buy items.
  • Gems buy runes.
  • Runes give special powers.
  • And finally: acclaim gives friends who hurt enemies and generate more acclaim.

Thats about it. Thanks for visiting!
